Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together and my services

What types of projects do you take on?

I specialize in frontend web development, UI/UX design, and responsive website creation. This includes portfolio websites, e-commerce platforms, web applications, landing pages, and custom web solutions. I'm particularly experienced with luxury brands and premium digital experiences.

What is your typical process for a new project?

My process typically involves: 1) Initial consultation to understand your needs, 2) Proposal and planning phase, 3) Design and prototyping, 4) Development and implementation, 5) Testing and revisions, 6) Launch and deployment, 7) Post-launch support. I maintain transparent communication throughout the entire process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple portfolio website might take 2-3 weeks, while a complex e-commerce platform could take 6-8 weeks. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I offer various support packages including monthly maintenance, security updates, content updates, and performance optimization. I also provide training materials and documentation so you can manage basic updates yourself if preferred.
